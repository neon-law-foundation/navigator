The Salt Lake City Nebula show-and-tell is for lawyers and legal professionals who want a grounded conversation about AI
in practice: where it saves time, where it needs review, and where our professional duties shape the workflow.

Come ready to compare notes. Beginners and advanced users are both welcome.

## Format

* Community demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Discussion of confidentiality, verification, and review
* Practical examples from legal drafting, intake, research, and operations
* Open time for questions and local connection

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
