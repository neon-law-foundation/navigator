Nebula show-and-tells are informal working sessions for the legal community. Bring a workflow, a prompt, a tool, a
client-safe review pattern, or a question you want to test with peers.

The Portland stop is built for lawyers, legal operations teams, founders, and legal-aid builders who want practical ways
to use AI without losing professional judgment. Come for the whole session or drop in between calls.

## Format

* Open floor demos and discussion
* Short workflow walkthroughs from local practitioners
* Practical guardrails for hallucination, confidentiality, review, and handoff
* Time to meet other lawyers building with AI

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
