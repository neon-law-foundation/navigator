The Boston Nebula show-and-tell is a practical working session: demos, workflows, prompts, and candid peer review for
lawyers and legal professionals exploring AI adoption.

Bring a use case or come listen. Either way, the focus is concrete practice and professional guardrails.

## Format

* Community-led workflow demos
* Discussion of intake, drafting, review, research, and operations
* Guardrails for confidentiality, accuracy, and attorney oversight
* Time to meet other legal builders

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
