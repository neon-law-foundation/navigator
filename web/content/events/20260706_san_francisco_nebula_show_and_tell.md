The San Francisco Nebula show-and-tell is a practical room for lawyers, founders, and legal professionals comparing real
AI workflows and the review habits that make them safe enough for client work.

Bring a workflow, a prompt, a demo, or a question. You can also come to listen and compare notes with the local legal
technology community.

## Format

* Community demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Open discussion for beginners and advanced users
* Guardrails for confidentiality, verification, review, and handoff
* Time to meet other lawyers building with AI

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
