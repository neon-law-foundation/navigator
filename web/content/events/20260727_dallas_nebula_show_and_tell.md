The Dallas Nebula show-and-tell brings lawyers and legal professionals together to compare concrete AI workflows, review
practices, and adoption lessons from the field.

Bring a demo if you have one. Bring a question if you are still sorting out where AI belongs in your practice.

## Format

* Workflow demos from community members
* Open discussion across practice areas
* Guardrails for confidentiality, accuracy, review, and escalation
* Time to meet other legal builders

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
