The Las Vegas Nebula show-and-tell brings Nevada's legal community together for a practical afternoon of AI workflows,
demos, and candid discussion about professional responsibility.

Bring what you are trying, what surprised you, and what still feels risky. The point is useful practice, not hype.

## Format

* Workflow demos from community members
* Open discussion for legal AI beginners and advanced users
* Guardrails for hallucination, confidentiality, review, and client handoff
* Time to meet other lawyers and legal builders

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
