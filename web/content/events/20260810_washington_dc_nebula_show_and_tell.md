The Washington, DC Nebula show-and-tell brings legal professionals together around practical AI workflows, review
habits, and access-to-justice questions.

The format is intentionally open: demos, peer review, and practical questions from people doing the work.

## Format

* Workflow demos from community members
* Discussion of responsible adoption in legal practice
* Guardrails for confidentiality, verification, review, and client handoff
* Time to meet other legal builders

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
