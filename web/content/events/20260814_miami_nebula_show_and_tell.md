The Miami Nebula show-and-tell is an open room for lawyers and legal professionals working through how AI fits into
client service, document work, intake, research, and operations.

You can present, listen, or bring a question you want peers to pressure-test.

## Format

* Practical demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Peer discussion across practice areas
* Guardrails for review, confidentiality, hallucination, and escalation
* Food, refreshments, and flexible drop-in timing

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
