The Kansas City Nebula show-and-tell is a practical working session: local demos, workflow walkthroughs, and peer review
for legal professionals exploring AI adoption.

Come to present, listen, or ask the questions that only show up once a tool meets real work.

## Format

* Community-led workflow demos
* Discussion of legal drafting, intake, research, and operations
* Guardrails for confidentiality, verification, and staff review
* Time for local connection

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
