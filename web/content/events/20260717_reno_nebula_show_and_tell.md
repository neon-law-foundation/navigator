Reno is a natural stop for Nebula: Nevada lawyers, founders, and public-interest builders in one room, trading what is
actually working and where the risks still need sharper guardrails.

This is not a lecture. It is a show-and-tell. Bring a demo, a workflow, a question, or a story from practice.

## Format

* Short demos from community members
* Open floor conversation about AI in legal practice
* Practical review patterns for documents, intake, research, and client communication
* Food, refreshments, and room to step out as work requires

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
