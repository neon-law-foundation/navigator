The Los Angeles Nebula show-and-tell is for lawyers and legal professionals who want a candid, practical conversation
about how AI fits into real client service.

The room is open to first-time users and advanced builders alike. Bring something to show, or bring the hard questions
you want peers to pressure-test.

## Format

* Short demos from community members
* Discussion of intake, drafting, review, research, and operations
* Guardrails for confidentiality, verification, and attorney oversight
* Flexible drop-in time for busy calendars

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
