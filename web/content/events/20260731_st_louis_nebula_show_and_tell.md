The St. Louis Nebula show-and-tell is a low-key room for practical AI work in law: demos, workflow sharing, review
patterns, and questions from people doing the work.

Bring your own example or come to compare notes with lawyers and legal professionals across the region.

## Format

* Practical demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Open discussion for beginners and advanced users
* Guardrails for confidentiality, verification, review, and client handoff
* Food, refreshments, and flexible attendance

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
