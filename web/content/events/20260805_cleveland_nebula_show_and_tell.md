The Cleveland Nebula show-and-tell is a practical session for lawyers and legal professionals exploring where AI fits
inside real legal work.

You can show a workflow, ask for feedback, or listen for patterns worth testing in your own practice.

## Format

* Community demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Peer discussion across practice areas
* Guardrails for confidentiality, accuracy, review, and escalation
* Time for local connection

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
