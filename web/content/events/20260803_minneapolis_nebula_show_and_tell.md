The Minneapolis Nebula show-and-tell is for legal professionals who want practical examples instead of abstract AI
adoption talk.

Bring a demo, a prompt, a workflow, or a hard question. The room is designed for candid peer learning.

## Format

* Local demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Discussion of confidentiality, verification, and review
* Practical examples from drafting, intake, research, and operations
* Time to meet other legal builders

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
