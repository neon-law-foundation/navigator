The Raleigh Nebula show-and-tell is a practical room for lawyers, legal operations teams, founders, and legal-aid
builders comparing AI workflows and review guardrails.

Bring a workflow if you have one. Bring skepticism if you need it. The point is useful peer learning.

## Format

* Local demos and workflow walkthroughs
* Open discussion for early and advanced users
* Guardrails for confidentiality, verification, and staff review
* Time to meet other legal builders in the region

## About Neon Law Nebula

Nebula is the Neon Law Foundation's public learning surface: workshops, presentations, and show-and-tells for lawyers
and legal professionals building with Neon Law Navigator and adjacent AI tools.
