## What the bylaws cover

- **Purpose** — the charitable mission the Foundation is organized to serve.
- **Membership** — whether the Foundation has voting members, and on what terms.
- **Board of directors** — size, election, terms, and removal of directors.
- **Officers** — the offices, how officers are chosen, and their duties.
- **Meetings** — notice, quorum, and how the board acts between meetings.
- **Committees** — any standing committees and the authority delegated to them.
- **Conflicts of interest** — how the board handles a director's competing
  interest (see also the [Conflict of Interest Policy](/foundation/transparency/conflict-of-interest)).
- **Amendments** — how the bylaws themselves may be changed.
