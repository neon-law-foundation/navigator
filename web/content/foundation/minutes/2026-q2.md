## Open-Source Publication of Neon Law Navigator

The board discussed the Foundation's work to publish
[neon-law-foundation/navigator](https://github.com/neon-law-foundation/navigator) as open-source software. The board
discussed the goal of making Navigator available so legal aid organizations can study it, adapt it, and operate it for
their own communities.

The board discussed the relationship between the open-source project and the Foundation's access-to-justice mission,
including the importance of clear licensing, implementation documentation, and a public project history that other
organizations can evaluate.

## Design-Partner Work

The board discussed ongoing work with several design partners. The board noted that design-partner feedback should help
the Foundation test Navigator's assumptions against real legal-services workflows, improve the software for
organizations with different operating models, and keep the project grounded in the needs of the people it is intended
to serve.
