## What the policy covers

- **Who is covered** — directors, officers, and other key persons who can influence a Foundation decision.
- **What counts as a conflict** — a financial or personal interest that competes with the Foundation's own interest.
- **Duty to disclose** — a covered person must disclose the interest before the board acts.
- **Recusal** — the interested person leaves the room for the deliberation and the vote.
- **Determination** — the disinterested directors decide whether the arrangement is fair to the Foundation.
- **Documentation** — the disclosure, the discussion, and the decision are recorded in the
  [board minutes](/foundation/transparency#minutes).
