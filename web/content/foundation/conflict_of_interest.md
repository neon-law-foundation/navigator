## What the policy covers

- **Who is covered** — directors, officers, and other key persons who can
  influence a Foundation decision.
- **What counts as a conflict** — a financial or personal interest that could
  compete with the Foundation's interest in a transaction or arrangement.
- **Duty to disclose** — a covered person must disclose the interest before the
  board acts.
- **Recusal** — the interested person leaves the room for the deliberation and
  the vote.
- **Determination** — the disinterested directors decide whether the
  arrangement is fair and in the Foundation's best interest.
- **Documentation** — the disclosure, the discussion, and the decision are
  recorded in the [board minutes](/foundation/transparency#minutes).
