# A prenup or uncontested divorce, handled with care

When two people have already agreed on the terms, the paperwork should not be the hard part. Neon Law Newleaf is a
flat-fee family-law service for either a prenuptial agreement or an uncontested divorce: a licensed attorney prepares
the agreed documents so you can put the terms in writing and move forward.

[[pricing]]

Try Newleaf when the hard conversation is already done. You have agreed on the terms. What remains is to write them
plainly, sign them correctly, and move forward without turning agreement into a fight.

Newleaf covers one of two matters: a prenuptial agreement before marriage, or an uncontested divorce after you have
already agreed to part ways. A licensed attorney prepares the agreed documents. For an uncontested divorce, we file the
dissolution and see the matter through to the decree.

Because even an amicable family-law matter can be legally adverse, we represent both of you only with informed written
consent. The conflict waiver says the firm represents you jointly, cannot advocate for one of you against the other, and
keeps no secrets between you. If a real disagreement opens, Newleaf stops and each of you should get independent
counsel.

The $555 fee covers the agreed prenup or uncontested dissolution. Court filing fees and service costs pass through at
cost. We do not handle negotiation, litigation, emergency relief, or contentious family-law work inside this flat-fee
service.

A written engagement letter states the exact scope, the fee, the waiver, and the terms for ending the work. If you are
ready to put agreement in writing, email **[support@neonlaw.com](mailto:support@neonlaw.com)** with a short note about
your situation.
