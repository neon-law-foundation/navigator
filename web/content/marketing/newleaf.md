# A prenup or uncontested divorce, handled with care

When two people have already agreed on the terms, the paperwork should not be the hard part. Neon Law Newleaf is a
flat-fee family-law service for either a prenuptial agreement or an uncontested divorce: a licensed attorney prepares
the agreed documents so you can put the terms in writing and move forward.

[[pricing]]

**How it works.** Tell us which Newleaf matter you need: a prenuptial agreement before marriage, or an uncontested
divorce after you have already agreed to part ways. A licensed attorney prepares the agreed documents. For an
uncontested divorce, you review and sign, and we file the dissolution with the court and see the matter through to the
decree.

**What $555 covers.** Preparing your prenuptial agreement or preparing and filing your uncontested dissolution — one
flat legal fee. For a divorce, the court's filing fees and any service-of-process costs pass through at cost in addition
to the flat fee.

**We represent both of you — with a conflict waiver.** Even an amicable family-law matter can be legally adverse, so
representing both partners is something the law allows only with your informed, written consent. You both sign a
conflict waiver up front: the Firm represents the two of you jointly, prepares only the prenup or dissolution you have
already agreed on, cannot advocate for either of you against the other, and keeps no secrets between you. If a real
disagreement comes up — anything about property, debts, support, children, or the terms of the prenup — the matter is no
longer a Newleaf matter, the Firm steps back from both of you, and each of you should get your own counsel. You are each
encouraged to consult independent counsel before signing the waiver.

**Non-contentious matters only.** Newleaf is for a prenuptial agreement or an uncontested divorce. We do not handle
contentious family-law matters. If your matter needs negotiation, litigation, emergency relief, or advocacy by one
partner against the other, we refer you to counsel outside this flat-fee service.

**Engagement letter governs.** A written engagement letter signed before we start states the exact scope, the $555 fee,
the conflict waiver you both sign, and how either of us can end the representation. Before that letter goes out we run a
conflict check against every current and former matter at the firm. If we cannot properly take your matter, we tell you
promptly and refer you to counsel outside this flat-fee service. There are no referral fees between Neon Law and any
firm we refer matters to.
